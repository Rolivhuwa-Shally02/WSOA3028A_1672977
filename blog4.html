How does the Medium is the message impact our understanding of the web?

April 1, 2021 by Mandaha RS

“The medium is the message”, is a statement by Marshall McLuhan, a philosopher who studied media theory, it was published
in his first chapter of The Extensions of Man in 1964. When we hear the word “Medium” we can think of media and
how the information is carried out to the society either through television, newspaper, radio, internet etc so it means.
the way we send and receive information. In this century we tend to use the internet more and it has a huge influence
in our daily lives, which is also a medium where we can obtain and put information. The web is part of the internet
from the word World Wide Web (WWW) which is composed of websites and webpages that we use
for entertainment, communication, advertisements, search information, putting information etc.
The question now is, Is the medium still the message? findings and my experience on using media in this digital world show
that indeed medium is still the message. Information from Television will not be received the same as the information
from Twitter and it will not give the same influence or receive the same results from the viewers. Another simple example
is marketing for a job on Facebook and Instagram, the response or interaction with businesses will not be the same on those
social platforms although the information you sent out is the same. In some situation people tend to take news or a report
on something seriously depending on the media which carried out the information for instance, the bigger/popular the website
the more the information spreads and a good example of the sharing website is YouTube, an incident happens in one country
we see many YouTubers from different countries posting a video about it so that shows that medium
seems to be valued more than the message.
Today’s world is driven by technology, the web is just part of it, and it will continue to be part of our lives, people are just moving
with change and that is the reason the form of media is rated depending on how many people use it on daily lives, the influence
it has on the society, its characteristics in general. Looking at the web again, the way someone experience a particular
website or web page will be different from my experience. The other thing is that if websites carry out information, our focus is
not going to continue being on the message but the website itself, human taking control of the web, using it for their
own purposes, reason we can now create our own websites through the internet, so again Medium is still the message.
