Development of technology and Websites

March 28, 2021 by Mandaha RS

We are living in a world of technology categorised into construction, transportation, energy, manufacturing,
bio-related and communication. This all brought into action by knowledge, theoretical and practical work of a man.
Technology also led to the development of websites that we are using now as a form of searching information and creating
site of learning, exploring and for entertainment. Findings show that the first website was created in 1989 by a British
scientist called Tim Berners-Lee who invented the World Wide Web then it was published in 1991. In this current
year we have large number of websites that are in use and other websites are still being created. We have seven top visited
websites namely Youtube.com, Fcebook.com, Wikipedia, Twitter, Amazon, play.google.com and lastly Instagram.
Websites are very useful and important in our daily lives and we spend more of time on the internet especially now during the pandemic.
We learn online thus we do more searches than relying on lecture notes or just textbooks. People advertise their businesses
or search for jobs on internet sites and that makes life easier considering that finding jobs or putting your work out
there for people to see and purchase would be harder if one had to travel to places and showcase or seek jobs.

Misuse of technology and Cybercrime

Technology offers a lot of services that end up causing addiction and abuse of the devices and its services, People end up using the internet
too much often, always on the phone or looking at the computer screen more hours than they normally should which is not healthy.
Another thing is when user’s personal information gets exposed which puts their lives at risk and this is due to software failure from the development
site. An example would be from 2018 standard security issue by Google+ and Facebook where too many people’s personal information
was exposed. Cyberbullying is also a big problem that even causes loss of lives and this is all misuse of technology.

Using technology and websites to improve our lives

As it should be technology started with the whole purpose of improving people’s lives and growing our world’s economy.
We should make use of the opportunity to grow our lives, develop, make money and create opportunities for others too.
