study of information geographies

March 28, 2021 by Mandaha RS


To understand the meaning of information geographies and its importance, we must first get an insight of what geography is, it is not just a subject after
all right? But there is more into it. Geography is the study of the earth’s physical features, atmosphere, spatial and geographic
data and how humans interact with that information. It also includes interaction and distribution of the population in the
specific region with the resources, economic and political activities. Information geographies is basically about the
knowledge of where something is, what is provided in the place, this can be presented in a form a map
with grids, lines, points and areas showing real life features in a specific area for instance
Johannesburg mining site and how much gold is available using polygons (areas). The study of information geography shows that “information has always had geography”, information changes as time and space change, it is mostly moderated by technology
and this is because we live in a world of technology, technology keeps on growing or improving while this growth is not the same
in every region of the world, some countries are not technologically developed as much therefore information will not be
transferred around effectively. Another way of referring information is through infrastructures and networks.
There are also (im)mutable augments on how information about places is essential because it affects accessibility and
understanding of different places of the world which in my opinion it is true, if a company or you as
an individual what to find information about a certain area for business or travelling purposes,
getting access of data on the search about the place is important.

Relation between Geographies access, enablement and participation

As a result of technology and services distribution, we get accessibility to transmission of information, participation of the people in that area and representation
of data. This is more about engagement and accessibility, how often do people in that area use internet,
do they have internet connection, Is there availability of devices to use different software applications
or technology tools, what about affordability? African countries do not have access to some technology
tools that are in china, therefore the use of these software tools will not be the same in each region
of the earth, participation is based on access and enablement.

Reflection on study geographies

This study shows the reason behind the uneven geographies of different spaces on earth is that lack of participation and representation rate just keep on increasing,
there is not much evidence showing that world’s information geographies is improving, Transmission of information and
improvement on technology and mobility expands more in urban areas. As the environment transforms, information also changes
