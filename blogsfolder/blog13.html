Critical analysis of my UX and UI: My Website

june 04, 2021 by Mandaha RS


Through readings and what I have learnt during lectures, UX and UI design are the foundation of content development. I will be discussing and giving an insight of what
I have done on my site, the design pattern and the reasons for them as well as what I do not see fit or bad
design ideas on my website and furthermore what I would want to correct that like part of bad design pattern
and for later improvements.
As part of my webstyle I decided to use colour codes that are not so into your face, the choice of my colours came from strawberries and blueberries, weird right?
But because my website is about food and my character loves those fruits, I made a choice to give my website a shade of
those colours. What I would want to change is the home page picture, and rather have berries that match up with my choice
of colours instead of lemons, however the flower in the background symbolises sanity and lemons for health while food is also
for health therefore that is the reason behind the homepage design. For more navigation through the website,
there is a hovering button in the middle of the homepage picture for more food ideas, I made this to be responsive
as part of user interface, users being able to interact with items on the website.

The header has the navigation menu and one dropdown menu, in the beginning it was two drop-down menus, but I chose to do something different for the users by using
buttons within another page to navigate to other 3 pages with fall under that one page where the buttons are located.
User experience will depend on the users find the design to be however my idea was made to interest those who will use the sight.
The drop-down menu and buttons shares the same colour shading as the footer and the header, this is so that the use of
colours correspond or match even on different pages. The header has a logo and brand next to each other, the logo was made
to be small because already there is a brand name otherwise it was going to be too much
and might cause bad design layout.


The theory blog page has sections of columns, with picture next to each blog content space however the blogs navigate to their respective pages using “read more” link.
This was done so that the blogs are accessible also on their own page there are at their own space and readable,
this made a great layout for information is clear and well conveyed. The theory blogs page with these columns can however
be improved for spaces in between are not therefore this interferes with the layout. The wireframes
are simple, easy to read although, there don not offer much information yet while this I being modified to set clarity on high
level to the users. The choice on footer colour was so it matches with the header, the are contacts icons and information,
cell number, location and email then just below for more contact information social icons
are used which are responsive when hovering they transform size, this part of design is to delight the users,
part of great experience.
