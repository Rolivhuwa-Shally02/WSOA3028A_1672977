Google plus and Facebook security failure leading to user’s data getting exposed

CYBERCRIME

April 15, 2021 by Mandaha RS


We live in a world of technology where the internet provides us with so many advantages in this modern world, we google information
and put information there, we have access to job advertisements, creation of websites and visiting other websites and there are Social
platforms for communication and entertainment. Facebook and Google are technology companies that provide social networks,
while the internet brings so many good things it also creates problems for the user’s in terms of privacy and security. Following the news
that made headlines in 2018 where Google+ discovered a security bug in March 2018 but revealed to the consumers in October 2018 and this
affected 52.5 million users. Although Google company mentioned that the financial and passwords information is not to be accessed by the
third-party applications there is still a risk of other developers getting access through google “sign in” we do on those other applications.
People’s lives were put to risk because Google+ apps has access to their users’ information through
the API. Some of the ethical issues involved in this software failure will be discussed as we proceed.
In April 2018, another social network made headlines when it was discovered that private information of users was accessed by Cambridge
Analytica for advertisement purposes during United States election in 2016. Cambridge Analytica is British political data-analysis
firm that is no longer in action. Facebook disclosed about 87 million users’ data without their knowledge and this was
reported to be a security breach tied to Cambridge Analytica following Trump’s 2016 elections. Facebook Inc.
Chief Executive Officer Mark Zuckerberg admitted to the reports however he was not aware of such and apologised for
not protecting users’ information as he should have.

The two social platforms discussed above are used in our daily lives and aim to bring a lot advantages in this modern world and improve people’s lives
and therefore they should bring forward their consumer’s need and protect their private information. The other issues we end up
questioning is who is benefiting from the leaked society’s private information and we end up no getting answers which shows no
respect to people’s confidential and privacy, later this can bring harm to people for they will no longer be safe from cybercriminals
or even stalkers. For future work developers need to address security during development not after releasing their products, they need
to maintain their security standards. They must give assurance by actions taken to users that if information in encrypted I should be hard to have
access to private information and also that if sensitive information is not to be
accessed by third-party developers then they should deny them access.

Both social networks admitted to the software failures and took responsibility of their involvement leakage of users’ personal data,
both in process of implementing new features with higher standard security and maintaining the current applications that had faults.
Although it is difficult for people to trust both Google and Facebook, investigation show that they are following requirements to
gain users trust back and in improvements done it shows that ethics are being practiced accordingly but this does not guarantee
they will not be any other Software failures however we hope for better improvements in security
by the technical companies and the developers.
