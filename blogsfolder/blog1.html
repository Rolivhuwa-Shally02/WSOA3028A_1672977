Scientific research and development

March 21, 2021 by Mandaha RS


Science is the most prodigious channel of knowledge and has a great impact on humanity. The development of science
started back in the centuries where Scientists would take their time to come up with solutions to create an advanced living
as it is now, we now live in a world of technology because of the scientific research Recording, storing and communication
is made to be effective using phones and cameras. This progression involves studies that we now call engineering and science.
The process might have been slow then but now that we have more access to resources, many researchers and developers
are involved to release more advanced products or technology items.

Looking into photography and film making, the use of cameras

The first camera was invented in 1888 by George Eastman and he named it “Kodak”. Studies show that cameras are made of cover
which has a compound containing glass fibre, therefore this indeed proves that manufacturing of cameras involves more of scientific
research including calculations and that is “Mathematics”. Back then a camera was considered a big machine which no one could
always carry around but due to the progression in research and work in the creation of cameras they’ve created portable
items we take around and use in taking pictures later storing data and keeping the records.
Long hours and months in construction of this machines with interchangeable parts brought us not only affordable items but reliable products
that are effective. I mean look at how small a camera is now but it does a lot of work, stores a lot memories and it is even creating opportunities
of jobs but mostly we don’t think of the processin making this cameras nor how it works therefore looking into this research brings a
lot of understanding in development of the world we are living in. Today we make stories and writings by using photography,
the movies or stories that we watch were created by these cameras in the production of film making which is now a course being studied
in universities, with just one click on the button, the lenses are on focus to take pictures or record, and this is great work of knowledge
. The research in the creation of cameras continues as it must to manufacture much better cameras, faster and with better lenses.

continuation of scientific research and other devices manufactured

On the positive side the studies must continue, looking at devices we use in everyday life scientific research had and still has great impact in creating
better and less stressful lives especially as the population continues to grow, for an instant creation of devices or machines in health facilities
that make job easier and faster. The problem is the misuse of technology and science for one person’s benefit that
affects a lot of people in negative way but looking at more benefits to humanity the development must continue.
