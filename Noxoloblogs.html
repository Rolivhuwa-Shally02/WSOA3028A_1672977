Focusing on your Health, Fitness and Sanity

April 15, 2021 by Mphephu NF


Healthy does not look a certain way, healthy is not a particular body type, healthy looks different on everybody. When you
take care of your health it is not just the outside look that matters but most importantly youremotional and mental well-being.
Physical activities like working out, jogging, hiking, dancing can help improve your physical well-being, improve your
emotional health and furthermore it decreases stress, anxiety and also improve your mood and that is a way of dealing
with mental health. You must know your body and be conscious of changes that you encounter or feel, know when you
are not well and seek help and this either emotionally, physically or mentally. Health also has to do with the food you
eat, are you eating healthy? Are you drinking water regularly? This kind of lifestyle is open for anyone in other ways it is not
expensive lifestyle to stay healthy, watching what you take inside your body is way of looking after your health.

Fitness is being physically fit and healthy, It has to do more with muscular strength, being able to perform certain activities
that require the use of strength, doing daily activities and this can be achieved by proper nutrition, exercise and getting
enough rest. There are four types of fitness that is, flexibility, balance, strength and endurance and being able to work on
just one of them promotes your body to be fit. Being fit is another way of taking care of your health, it improves your quality of life,
increase energy, boost immune system and it helps your body to fight diseases or reduce the risk of developing diseases.

sanity, you must find what works for you in dealing with your mental health however there are common activities that can help like
exercising, eating healthy, enjoying doing your hobbies, get busy on activities that release stress, play games, spend time with friends,
family or people who give you peace, having peace comes from taking care of your sanity as well. It is important to take care of your
sanity, some people find sanity from simple things such as flowers, being close to flowers, watering them and viewing nature itself,
photography (capturing moments) also works for some people, staying indoors watching movies,doing work because some people
surprisingly love working, being on their computer and programming or doing other stuff that keep them busy.

When it comes to mental health you find what works for you, pay attention of how you are reasoning especially when going through
a lot or overworking and learn to do simple things like getting fresh air, go for a walk and also develop the habit of being positive, if
things not working out causes you stress, follow the tips above or do what works for you and stay healthy.
